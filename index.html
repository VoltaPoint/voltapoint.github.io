INTRODUCTION

What our project is about

What is Greenhouse?

A greenhouse is a structure with a plastic, mesh, polycarbonate roof and its interior heated/ventilated together with natural sunlight to warm / cool the plants, soil, and other plants inside the building

Definition of Smart Green House

A smart greenhouse is a greenhouse that uses automative and sensors to monitor and control the environment for growing crops

The goal is to create an ideal growing condition for the plants.

Problem Statement

    🌱 Challenges in Traditional Greenhouse Management

    🌱 Limitations of Traditional Greenhouse Systems

    🌱 Relating to the SDG2(The Sustainability Development Goal) issue

OBJECTIVE

Our objective

To develop a smart greenhosue system that utilize IoT technolgy to automate and optimize environmental conditions for plant growth

To monitor key environmental parameters such as soil moisture, air humidity, temperature, light intensity, and pH levels using sensors integrated with a Rasberry Pi Pico W microcontroller

To design an automated irrigation system that activates pumps based on soil moisture levels and temperature conditions, ensuring efficient water usage

Shading System: A static shade net protects plant from excessive sunlight.
