What is Greenhouse?

A greenhouse is a structure with a plastic, mesh, polycarbonate roof and its interior heated/ventilated together with natural sunlight to warm / cool the plants, soil, and other plants inside the building

Definition of Smart Green House

A smart greenhouse is a greenhouse that uses automative and sensors to monitor and control the environment for growing crops

The goal is to create an ideal growing condition for the plants.
